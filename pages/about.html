About Work Not Work Resume

Hi, I'm Melissa.

I'm a manufacturing and process engineer based in San Francisco. I spent seven years at Apple working on hardware products — the kind of work that lives at the intersection of design and production. Developing the processes that turn designs into physical parts on the factory floor, bridging manufacturability feedback to product designers and getting things across the finish line to customers. And lots of troubleshooting along the way.

I'm currently on a career break. After getting married and spending 6 months traveling with my wife, I'm now in the process of applying for my green card, and taking time to think about what's next. I'm drawn to manufacturing operations and increasingly to AI — particularly the program and operations side, where there's real opportunity to put my production expertise to work in a new field.

Outside of work I ride bikes, grow gourmet mushrooms (and occasionally forage for them), and tend an ever-expanding garden. I'm originally from just outside Toronto but San Francisco has been home long enough that I complain about the fog with genuine feeling. I love it here and traveling this past summer only confirmed it: there's no city I'd rather come back to.

Thanks for taking the time to check out my website — like myself, it is a work in progress. I'm happy to connect and talk work, interests, or anything in between. There are some options for getting in touch in the top right.
