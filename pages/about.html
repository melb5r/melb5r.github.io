About Work Not Work Writing Resume

Hi, I'm Melissa.

I'm a manufacturing and process engineer based in San Francisco. I spent seven years at Apple working on hardware products — the kind of work that lives at the intersection of design and production, bridging manufacturability feedback to product designers and getting things across the finish line to customers. Process development, quality systems, cross-functional coordination, getting things shipped.

I'm currently on a career break. I've been travelling, applying for my green card, and taking time to think about what's next. I'm drawn to manufacturing operations and increasingly to AI — particularly the program and operations side, where there's real opportunity to put hard-won production expertise to work in a new field.

Outside of work I ride bikes, grow gourmet mushrooms (and occasionally forage for them), and tend an ever-expanding garden with ambitions toward a small food forest. I try to keep up with home projects too — most recently building an outdoor sauna. I'm originally from just outside Toronto — University of Waterloo, chemical engineering. San Francisco has been home long enough that I complain about the fog with genuine feeling. This past summer my wife and I spent four months travelling Europe, and somehow that only confirmed it: there's no city we'd rather come back to.
